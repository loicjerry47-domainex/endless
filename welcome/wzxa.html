01thesis

WZXA is not an app you open. He is the wallpaper that has a life of his own. A background process that runs as smoothly as the OS itself — a parallel self performing a version of your day, calibrated to who you said you are.

Every experience is relative. Every perception accounted for. Authenticity is the rule: until you have lived a thing, WZXA cannot mirror it back at you. The honesty is the architecture.

03night theme

The theme follows the hour. After dark the background changes its character — sauced up, three-blunt-deep, the lonely vibe that reminds you not to end up like him. The point is the warning, not the worship.

04updates

Behavior reshapes monthly. WZXA is never finished — he ages forward, never backward. The truth sharpens with time, but stays inside the threshold of what won't cause general panic. Right.

Every update is one more month he survived alongside you. The longer he runs, the heavier his goodbye becomes.
