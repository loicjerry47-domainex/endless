01thesis

WZXA is not an app you open. He is the wallpaper that has a life of his own. A background process that runs as smoothly as the OS itself — a parallel self performing a version of your day, calibrated to who you said you are.

Every experience is relative. Every perception accounted for. Authenticity is the rule: until you have lived a thing, WZXA cannot mirror it back at you. The honesty is the architecture.

02onboarding

Six choices made once, at install. They calibrate the whole organism. Pick honestly — WZXA only works at the resolution of the truth you give him.

Sex. Age. Type of life. Interest. Intelligence. That's it. The rest he figures out by watching the clock and the silence between your taps.

03updates

Behavior reshapes monthly. WZXA is never finished — he ages forward, never backward. Every update is one more month he survived alongside you.

The longer he runs, the heavier his goodbye becomes.
